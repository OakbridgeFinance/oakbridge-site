What's Possible
What you can do with structured data.
Once your QBO data is flat and mapped to your structure, one dataset powers everything — executive dashboards, board decks, ad hoc analysis, all of it.
01 · Custom Reporting

Reports tailored to every audience.

Your CEO wants a clean P&L. Your investors want segment-level gross margin. Operations wants spend by category. Build a custom account map for each audience and generate the right view in one click.

02 · Dashboards

Plug straight into your dashboards.

The flat file structure works natively with Excel pivots, Tableau, Power BI, or Looker. Build dashboards on QBO data, or fold financials into a larger data model alongside your CRM, billing, or ops systems.

03 · Ad Hoc Analysis

Answer questions without rebuilding reports.

Need digital marketing spend trends for Business Unit A over the last 18 months? Pivot on it. Comparing Q1 professional fees across three entities? Filter and sort. Flat data means the question is always easier than the answer.

Trust, but verify.
Other tools pull your data into a black box. Acorn shows its work.

Every Acorn report includes an automatic reconciliation tab that checks every GL account against QBO's own reports — so you never have to wonder if the numbers are right. No other extraction tool does this.
